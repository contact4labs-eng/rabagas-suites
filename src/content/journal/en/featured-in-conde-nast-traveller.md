Condé Nast Traveller's editorial team included Rabagas Suites in their "On the Move" gallery of European destinations worth a journey. The piece highlights small, locally-rooted properties across the continent — places where a stay is something distinct from a stop. We were quietly pleased to find ourselves there.

We will go on doing what we have always done: opening six restored pre-1910 doors in the old quarter of Apollonia, walking guests to their suites in person, sharing the tavernas we eat at on our own evenings.
