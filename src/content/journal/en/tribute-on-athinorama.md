Athinorama — a Greek cultural and travel publication that has been guiding readers around Athens and the islands for forty years — included Rabagas Suites in their short list of places to stay on Sifnos for travellers looking for something quieter, more authentic, and more rooted in the village than a beachfront hotel.

The recommendation matters to us because Athinorama writes for a domestic Greek audience that knows the Cyclades in a different way than visitors do. To be picked by people who already know the islands is the kind of vote we trust.
